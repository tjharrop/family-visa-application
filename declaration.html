Your duty to give all reasons for staying in the UK

You must state in this application all your reasons or grounds for wishing to stay in the United Kingdom.

If your claim is refused, you could lose any right of appeal you may otherwise have qualified for if you do not state in this application any reasons or grounds for remaining in the UK that you later provide without good reason.

This is a requirement under section 120 of the Nationality, Immigration and Asylum Act 2002.
