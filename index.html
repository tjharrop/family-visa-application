Partner on the 5 or 10 Year Route

Introduction

Apply to extend your leave to remain as the partner of a settled person, or to switch your leave to remain to that of the partner of a settled person.

Biometric residence permit

As part of the application process, you are required to apply for a biometric residence permit. If you choose Standard or Priority service you will be required to pay an additional fee of £19.20 per applicant to have your biometrics collected.

Health Surcharge

You may have to pay a health surcharge in addition to your visa application fee. Check how much you may have to pay.

To apply for Family Routes you need:

  • to be at least 18 years old
  • to be photographed and have your fingerprints recorded
  • a dependant of someone with valid leave as a points-based system migrant
  • to speak English

With Family Routes you can:

  • stay in the UK for up to 30 months
  • leave and re-enter the UK
  • study in the UK
  • stay in the UK only for the same period as your partner
  • work in the UK
  • apply to stay longer in the UK
  • do business in the UK

You cannot:

  • work as a trainee Doctor or Dentist unless specified evidence is provided
  • receive public money, like unemployment or housing benefit

To apply for Family Routes you need to:

  • fill in the application form
  • have your documents ready
  • pay a health surcharge, if applicable
  • pay the application fee
  • if you choose Standard or Priority Service, we will notify you to go to the Post Office to give your biometrics. You must pay an additional fee of £19.20 per applicant for this
  • if you choose Premium Service, you will provide your biometrics at your Premium Service Centre appointment. You do not have to pay an additional fee

These fees do not include biometric residence permit or health surcharge fees.
